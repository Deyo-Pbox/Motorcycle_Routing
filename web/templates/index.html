📋 How to Use
1
Set Origin (A): Search for a location or click on the map to set your starting point.
2
Set Destination (B): Search for a location or click on the map to set where you want to go.
3
Add Waypoints (Optional): Click + Add Waypoint to add stops along the way.
4
Calculate Route: Click Calculate Motorcycle Route to find the best path.
5
Watch the Route: Click ▶ Play to see the motorcycle estimated ride and speed along the route. The color shows traffic: 🟢 Free, 🟡 Moderate, 🔴 Heavy.
💡 Tips
🔍 Searching is more accurate than clicking
🗺️ Click on map to quickly add waypoints
🗑️ Click × to remove a location and try again
⚠️ Out of Scope
The location you selected is outside the Camarines Sur service area.
📍 Service Area: Naga, Camarines Sur
